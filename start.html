Terms and Conditions

Design Process
  1. Client is to complete the Design Brief Below.
  2. I will email you a PayPal invoice that is to be paid prior to the start of any design work.
  3. The initial design concepts will be sent to you via email for approval.
  4. After the design draft is presented, the client is to provide feedback. We ask that the client consolidates all comments, ideas and questions together to constitute a complete round of revisions as there is a 3-round revision limit. After that additional revisions are $5 each.
  5. Upon approval of the design, I will email you the design in various file formats (JPG, PNG & PDF).
  6. The design copyright belongs to you the client, however I retain the right to display the design for personal promotion.
Billing & Payment
After completing the design brief below, the client is billed via PayPal invoice. I will not begin any design work until 100% of the payment is received.
Project Timeframe
The timeframe needed to complete the design varies with each client and greatly depends on the complexity of the design. Design turnaround time is usually within 10 business days, more or less. Additional rounds of revisions can increase this time.
Content Delivery
The design will be delivered in the following file formats:
  • JPG - has a solid background, good for the web and social media sites.
  • PNG - has a transparent background, ideal for the web.
  • PDF - great for printing.
Revisions
For all designs done by Shara Lue Designs, there is a limit of 3-rounds of revisions. A round of revisions may consist of a set of minor changes such as color, spelling, font etc.. Additional rounds of revisions may be purchased for $5 each.
Copyright Info
The design copyright belongs to you the client, however I retain the right to display the design for personal promotion. The actual copyrighting of the design is up to the client, check out my blog post on How to copyright your design for assistance.
Refunds & Cancellation
If for any reason the client decides to cancel the project, the client is unable to use the design and the copyright of all existing work belongs to Shara Lue Designs. There are absolutely no refunds once the design has been started.
